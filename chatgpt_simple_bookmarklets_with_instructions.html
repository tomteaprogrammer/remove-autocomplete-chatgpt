ChatGPT Simple Bookmarklets

To install, drag one of the buttons below to your bookmarks bar (or right-click → “Bookmark this link”). Once saved, clicking the bookmark on the target site will remove the unwanted <ul> and keep it gone.

ChatGPT Simple (Strict) ChatGPT Simple (Loose)
